Help us translate!

Since Onetime Secret launched in 2012, one of the most requested features has been to support languages other than english. Many visitors -- in fact, most -- live in countries where English is not the first or even second language.

One of the major goals of Onetime Secret is to simplify secure communication so that it can help as many people as possible. Translating the site plays a big role in that. Since 2015 our contributors have added over 20, plus corrections and other updates! But we still have a ways to go. I need your help to add support for more languages.
